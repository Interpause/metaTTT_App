Rules

  • There are 9 TicTacToe boards in a 3x3 grid. The goal is to win 3 boards in a line.
  • The player is limited to a board determined by the previous turn.
  • The position of the placed piece corresponds to the board used in the next turn.
  • The first player chooses the starting board.
  • If the placed piece corresponds with a finished board, the next player is allowed to choose which board to use next.

Thanks for playing!
